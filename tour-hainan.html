Trip highlights

Six days. Six unforgettable experiences.

⛵
Day 1 · Haikou

Haikou Bay Yacht Night Cruise

Depart from the historic Dongpo Old Wharf. Glide through the glittering city nightscape as skyscraper light shows reflect off the water. Cool sea breeze, dazzling lights, and the soul of coastal Haikou.

🛍️
Day 2 · Ocean Flower Island

Asia's Largest Duty-Free City

Haikou International Duty-Free City — 800+ brands, the world's single largest duty-free store. Then Ocean Flower Island's 6 themed commercial streets, 8 food zones, and the stunning evening light show.

🕌
Day 3 · Sanya

108m Nanshan Guanyin + Scenic Railway

Ascend to the feet of the world's tallest sea Guanyin statue. Then board the Sanya Tourist Railway through tropical coastal scenery to Tianya Town — blue-white buildings and ocean views like a Miyazaki world.

🐠
Day 4 · Sanya

Undersea Lunch · Atlantis · Phoenix Island

Lunch at Yalong Bay Undersea Restaurant surrounded by marine life. Discover Atlantis Sanya — the world's third Atlantis resort. Check into shell-shaped Phoenix Island for the unlimited seafood buffet and Iron Flower fire show.

🚁
Day 5 · Sanya → Haikou

Helicopter Flight + Li & Miao Village

Binglang Valley — China's first 5A ethnic cultural park, with original rainforest and live Li & Miao performances. Then a complimentary 1km low-altitude helicopter flight with 360° aerial views of Sanya.

☕
Day 2 · En route

Fushan Coffee Town

Hainan's most beautiful small town and China's No. 1 coffee town. Coffee and snack tasting included — the perfect midpoint between the duty-free city and Ocean Flower Island.
