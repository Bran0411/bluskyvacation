Who you'll learn from

Six world-class companies.
Six learning sessions.

🛒
Strategy & Culture

Alibaba Group

Inside Alibaba's Xixi Campus. Learn the "Three Axes" leadership framework — strategy, culture, people. Tour the cultural wall, Alibaba's growth milestones, and dine at the Alibaba canteen.

🚗
Smart Manufacturing

Geely EV Factory

Walk through the full stamping-welding-assembly line. See how a robot assembles a full electric vehicle in 6 minutes on Geely's AMA EV platform — one of China's most advanced.

🤖
Industrial AI

Shenhao Robotics 申昊科技

20+ years in fault detection. 3,000+ inspection robot deployments. Tour their new production quality science park — rail, power grid, emergency and marine inspection robots.

🧠
Brain-Computer Interface

BrainCo 强脑科技

The first Chinese team admitted to Harvard Innovation Lab. Their bionic hand — controlled by brainwaves — lit the torch at the 2022 Asian Para Games. Hands-on experience with BCI technology.

🎤
AI & Language

iFlytek 科大讯飞

China's leading AI speech and cognitive company. Test their live voice recognition accuracy, experience virtual human creation, and learn about the Spark LLM and its industry deployments.

🏙️
Future City

Robot Town 机器人小镇

China's first robot-named innovation district. 40+ robot products on display — dancing robots, calligraphy robots, chess robots, VR smart factories. Interactive experience across two floors.
