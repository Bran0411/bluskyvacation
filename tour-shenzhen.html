Who you'll learn from

Five AI pioneers.
One future city.

🔋
EV & Energy

BYD + Yunba

From battery startup to the world's largest EV maker. Walk through BYD Museum, hand-roll a battery cell as a souvenir, eat the legendary "Tongle Chicken Leg Rice," and see Yunba's autonomous rail system.

🤖
Humanoid Robots

UBTECH 优必选

Listed on Hong Kong Stock Exchange in 2023. Their Walker S2 robot performed at the 2025 National Games opening. See humanoid robots working in logistics, retail, education and smart manufacturing.

🚌
Smart City Transit

Shenzhen Bus Group

China's most advanced autonomous bus demonstration base. 20 driverless vehicles on active routes. Ride one. Understand how a city-scale AI traffic system operates and what it means for future urban mobility.

🚁
Drone Logistics

SF Fengyi 丰翼科技

China's first UAV operating license holder. Active in logistics, medical supply, emergency response across 12 countries. See drones launching from the rooftop and understand the full uncrewed logistics chain.

🚗
Autonomous Driving

Baidu Apollo 萝卜快跑

The world's largest autonomous driving operator and biggest 5G cloud fleet. Shenzhen is the first city to open self-driving taxis in its CBD. You'll ride one — 3 people per car, 5-minute technology park loop.

🌉
Iconic Experience

HZM Bridge Cruise + Ping'an Tower

Sail under the world's longest sea bridge at sunset — dusk + blue hour + bridge light-up. Earlier that evening, ascend to 547m at Ping'an Finance Centre Floor 116 for a 360° view of Shenzhen Bay and beyond.
